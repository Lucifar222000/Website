Our Story

Passion Plated,
Perfectly.

Founded by Chef Fikayo Adeyemi in 2010, Fiko's Place was born from a simple belief — that food should be a journey. Drawing from his grandmother's kitchen in Ibadan and a decade of training across Paris and Cape Town, Fikayo blends the soul of West Africa with the precision of modern fine dining.

Every dish at Fiko's Place is a conversation between tradition and innovation — using locally-sourced ingredients, seasonal produce, and time-honoured techniques elevated for the contemporary palate.
